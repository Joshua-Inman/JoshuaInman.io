A Little Bit About Myself

My name is Joshua Inman, I was born in Upland California on february 5th 1999. I'm currently eighteen years old and live in Peachtree City Georgia. When I’m not coding I enjoy playing video games, going to the gym, and doing various creative things such as art and video editing. I also love to travel to new places and eat delicious foods.

I started coding sometime around early June of 2017 and have loved it since. I really enjoy and appreciate the art of coding as it allows anyone to simply create or design whatever they can possible imagine. Unlike most things in life impeding you; anyone can sit down and open a text editor and start coding. I would tremendously recommend to anyone that has pondered the thought of learning to program or is even just a little curious about it.

At the moment I don't have many coding related projects that are publicly viewable, although you can always visit my Github profile page to see what I’m working on.

I also have a referral link if anyone is interested in signing up for Team TreeHouse which is where I've learned some of my programming knowledge. Also, my Instagram is @JoshuaJInman if you would like to follow me.
